About

I know how to deliver under pressure.

I am a Finland-based game and product leader who stays close to the work, from strategy and production planning to systems and interfaces.

My experience spans art, design, scripting, UI/UX, and game production. I also build hands-on, prototyping full-stack and using AI-assisted workflows to take ideas from concept to working software. I have led internal and outsourced teams, built long-term external partnerships, and helped projects move through difficult production challenges, major releases, and organizational change. I'm persistent, take calculated risks, and stay accountable when the path is uncertain.
