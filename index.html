About

I make complex products feel understandable.

I am a Finland-based game and product leader who stays close to the work, from strategy and production planning to systems and interfaces.

I have led internal and outsourced teams, built long-term external partnerships, and helped projects move through difficult production challenges, major releases, and organizational change. I bring persistence, calculated risk-taking, and the willingness to stay accountable when the path is uncertain.
